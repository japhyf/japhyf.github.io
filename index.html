ShutterMate

Photo Recognition App for Chess Board Analysis



ShutterMate is an app for chess players who perfer a physcial board, but still want to utilize the board analysis tools available only to digital players. ShutterMate allows these players to photograph their physcial board, convert it to a digital board, and analyze it with a built-in Stockfish engine.

FoodClues

Helping Low-Income Individuals Find Their Next Meal



FoodClues is a texting service and web app designed to help those reliant on Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) find their next meal. Anyone can text their current address to our secure line at +1 702 904 8384 to recieve the name, address, and current distance from the nearest EBT-accepting establishment.
